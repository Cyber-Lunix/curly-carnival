FAQ

How often should I use it?

Apply 2-3 times weekly for best results, or nightly for intensive care.

Are there allergens?

Check each product label — we avoid harsh preservatives but some botanical allergens may be present.

What are the main benefits of using hair growth oils?

Hair growth oils can stimulate follicles, improve scalp health, reduce breakage, and promote thicker, shinier hair through natural ingredients like rosemary and castor oil.

How do I apply the oil?

Apply a small amount to your scalp and massage gently. For best results, leave it on for 30-60 minutes or overnight, then shampoo as usual.

Is it suitable for all hair types?

Yes, our blends are designed for various hair types, but if you have specific concerns like sensitive scalp, consult a dermatologist first.

Are the ingredients natural?

Yes, we use pure, natural botanical extracts without synthetic chemicals. All ingredients are ethically sourced.

How long does it take to see results?

Results vary, but many users notice improvements in 4-8 weeks with consistent use. Hair growth is gradual.

Can I use it with other hair products?

Absolutely! Our oils work well with shampoos, conditioners, and styling products. Apply oil before or after other treatments as needed.
